I grew up in a small, rural town in Kentucky near Fort Knox. I moved with to the Denver area with my wife in 2013 because we both wished to persue oppurtunities in the cannabis industry. We now have a five year old son, two dogs and a house in Lakewood. I enjoy spending quality time with my family, making music with my friends, reading sci-fi novels and watching college sports and horror movies. I've played drums in punk/hardcore bands for a large part of my life starting at age 15.

I have been a jack of many trades throughout my life. I've done everything from factory production to barbering and I am now challenging myself by entering the world of web developement.
